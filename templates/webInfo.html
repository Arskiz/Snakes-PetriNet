About This Project

The Snakes CPN Viewer is a specialized tool for visualizing and analyzing Colored Petri Nets. It provides researchers and students with an intuitive interface to explore complex Petri net structures and their behaviors.

This viewer was developed for DIGIT at TU Clausthal to enhance research capabilities in formal modeling and verification methods.
